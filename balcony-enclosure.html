Project Overview

We designed and installed an aluminium balcony enclosure for a high-rise apartment in Karachi, providing safety and style. The enclosure allowed the client to enjoy their balcony year-round while maintaining an unobstructed view.

Key Features

  • Frameless glass panels for a seamless view.
  • Sturdy aluminium supports for safety.
  • Sliding panels for flexible ventilation.
  • Weatherproof seals for protection against rain and wind.
