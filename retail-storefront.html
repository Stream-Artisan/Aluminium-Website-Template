Retail Storefront

Custom aluminium storefront for a retail space in Karachi.

Project Overview

We created a custom aluminium storefront for a retail space in Karachi, designed to enhance visibility and attract customers. The storefront combined large glass panels with sturdy aluminium frames to create an inviting and modern appearance.

Key Features

  • Full-height glass panels for maximum visibility.
  • Brushed aluminium frames for a premium look.
  • Automatic sliding doors for ease of access.
  • UV-resistant coatings to protect against sun damage.
